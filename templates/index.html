Used car resale analysis

For example, input Brand: "Subaru", Model: "Forester", Zipcode: "07302", and then click "Query" button, to find out price depreciation as a function of mileage. For now, zipcode in NY, NJ, and CA are supported. More will be added. Displayed data are within 50 miles of the query zipcode. Click https://github.com/botaojia/cars to view source code and flow chart. Click here to see the full list of supported brand and models.

Displayed data are collected by crawling http://www.carsdirect.com every two weeks. All data points represent currently on market used cars.
